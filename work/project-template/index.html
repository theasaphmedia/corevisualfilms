The Morrisons — A Coastal Love Story

Client The Morrisons
Year 2024
Location Santorini, Greece
Duration 12 min

The Story

"Four continents. One love. A film that earns its place in a family's story forever."

When The Morrisons approached us, they had one request: make it feel like the best film they'd ever seen — and it happens to be about us. No pressure.

We spent three days in Santorini, two in New York, and a final weekend capturing the quiet moments — the ones that don't make it onto mood boards but end up being the shots you never stop watching. The golden hour at the cliffside. The hotel corridor at 2am. The father's face during the first dance.

The grade leans warm and slightly lifted — a nod to analogue film. Score: licensed original composition. Audio captured with dual-system sound.
